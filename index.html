DOSHA MATERNAL AND CHILD HEALTH CENTER/DMCHC

DOSHA Maternal and Child Health Center (DMCHC) is a privately owned center that provides comprehensive reproductive (obstetrics and gynecology) and child (pediatric) health service. The field of obstetrics and gynecology are the two surgical–medical specialties dealing with the female reproductive organs in their pregnant and non-pregnant state, respectively. The field is adept at the surgical and medical management of the entire scope of clinical pathology involving female reproductive organs, and to provide care for both pregnant and non-pregnant clients. On the other hand, the field of pediatrics is to provide care for both healthy and sick children including healthy life style promotion, disease prevention and curative care.
