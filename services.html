OUR SERVICE PACKAGES:

=>Gynecological service:

  • Outpatient level diagnostic and treatment service on gynecologic conditions.
  • Screening and ablative treatment for precancerous lesions of the cervix.
  • Training of individual client on self-examination of the breast for early detection of breast lump.
  • Minor gynecological procedure: cervical punch biopsy, endometrial biopsy, cryo-therapy, marcipulization for Bartholin’s abscess, treatment for imperforate hymen and other minor vaginal reconstructive surgery under local anaesthesia.
  • Major Gynaecologic operative care: Total Abdominal Hysterectomy, Myomectomy, Salpingo-oophorectomy, vaginal hysterectomy and anterior/posterior colporaphyfor pelvic organ prolapse, and other major gynaecologic surgery.
  • Treatment of peri/postmenopausal symptoms

=>Obstetric care:

  • Ante Natal Care (ANC) including PMTCT service
  • Care for other medical conditions complicating pregnancy
  • Delivery care: Spontaneous vaginal delivery, Assisted vaginal delivery, Caesarean section
  • Inpatient/Outpatient management of obstetric complications
  • Post Natal Care (PNC).
  • Post abortion care.
  • Comprehensive safe abortion care as far as permitted by law.
  • Treatment and follow-up care for molar pregnancy.
  • Diagnosis and treatment of ectopic pregnancy.

=>Family planning service:

  • Short acting methods(OCP, Injectable, Patches, male and female Condoms).
  • Implant insertion and removal.
  • IUD insertion and removal.
  • Tubal ligation by Minilapartomy under local anaesthesia.
  • Vasectomy under local anaesthesia.

=>Infertility work up and treatment at site and at referral centre

=>Counseling and treatment on sexuality:

  • Counseling and treatment on sexual dysfunction.
  • Counseling, investigation and treatment of STI.
  • Adolescent friendly reproductive health service.

=>Paediatric care:

  • Well baby clinic: growth monitoring, advise on breast feeding and healthy dieting, immunization.
  • Sick baby clinic: outpatient and inpatient care.
  • NICU-Neonatal Intensive Care Unit
  • Immunization services: National EPI package, TT, HBV, and HPV.

=>Service at Referral Hospital with which we shall have memorandum of understanding for collaborative care:

  • ICU care
  • Treatment of gynecologic cancers
  • Other service like pediatric surgery, medical, general surgical, dental, psychiatric care

=>Laboratory service:

  • Haematology: Hgb, Blood group and RH, CBC, Blood chemistry(Renal function test, Liver function test, Coagulation profile…) and Serology.
  • Wet mount and gram-stain of body fluids and discharge.
  • Urinalysis: Chemistry and microscopy.
  • Stool examination: Microscopy.

=>Imaging: Ultrasound

=>Pharmacy service: Level of hospital pharmacy as dispensary
